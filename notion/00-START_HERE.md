This is the Notion companion to The Job Hunter's AI Bundle. The PDF is the long-form reference; this workspace is the searchable, copy-paste, day-of-the-search version. Use whichever fits the moment.

## What's in here

- **Prompt library** — all 44 prompts, indexed by stage and module, ready to copy.
- **Templates** — 8 negotiation scripts, 3 worksheets (BATNA / comp research / walkaway math), 3 cover letter templates, plus checklists. Fillable.
- **Job tracker** — a database to log every role you apply to. Pre-seeded with two examples so you see the shape.
- **Progress checklist** — a 7-day plan that ties specific prompts to specific milestones.
- **Changelog** — version history. The bundle gets free updates for 12 months.

## 15-minute quick-win path

If you have an offer in hand and need to negotiate this week:

1. Open the **Prompt library**, jump to Module 5, run **Prompt 1 (Market-rate research)** with your role + level + city. (5 min)
2. Open **Templates**, fill out the **BATNA worksheet**. Honest answers, not aspirational. (5 min)
3. Run **Prompt 3 (Counter-offer scripting)** with the BATNA in hand. Paste the actual offer numbers. (5 min)

You now have a defensible counter-offer and the leverage map behind it.

Earlier in the search? Open the **Progress checklist** — it walks the bundle in the order most people need it.

## Three rules that make the prompts work

1. **Don't run them on a blank slate.** Paste your actual resume, the actual JD, your actual numbers.
2. **Run the BATNA worksheet before any negotiation prompt.** Module 5 prompts assume you've done it.
3. **The prompts are sharp; you have to feed them.** Generic input = generic output.
