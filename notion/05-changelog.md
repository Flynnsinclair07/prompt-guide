# Changelog

Version history for The Job Hunter's AI Bundle. The bundle gets free updates for 12 months from purchase. When ChatGPT changes — model upgrades, new context windows, behavior shifts — the prompts get re-tested and rewritten as needed. The same Gumroad download link picks up the new version. No re-purchase, no new email.

This page tracks what changed and when. New prompts, prompt fixes, format changes, and any v2 backlog items that get pulled forward. Older versions are archived (link at the bottom of each entry) in case you want the version you originally bought.

---

## v1.0 — 2026-05-17

**Initial release.**

- 5 modules · 44 prompts · 8 negotiation scripts · 3 worksheets
- 119-page PDF (hyperlinked TOC, code-styled prompt callouts)
- Notion companion workspace (this thing) — searchable prompt library, fillable templates, job tracker, 7-day progress checklist
- 14-day no-questions refund window via Gumroad
- 12 months of free updates from purchase

**Module breakdown:**

- **Module 1 — Resume**: 8 prompts (resume from scratch · rewrite · bullet rewrite · ATS keyword injection · quantify achievements · career-switcher · executive summary · LinkedIn → resume converter) + 5-step ATS optimization checklist + 3 before/after worked examples (entry-level grad, mid-career switcher, senior IC).

- **Module 2 — Cover Letter**: 6 prompts (hook-first opener · mirror-the-JD · industry-switcher · referral · cold application · follow-up) + 3 plug-and-play templates + "What hiring managers actually skim for" 13-signal cheat sheet.

- **Module 3 — Interview Prep**: 12 prompts (behavioral by role · technical by industry · case interview · STAR structuring · weakness reframe · "why this company" · closing questions · salary screen · panel prep · post-interview thank-you · reverse-interview · red-flag detection) + 3-round AI-grilling rehearsal workflow + answer-scoring prompt.

- **Module 4 — LinkedIn Profile**: 8 prompts (headline · About section · experience bullets · skills keywording · engagement-seed posts · recruiter cold-DM · recommendation request · post-connection follow-up) + 14-item optimization checklist + weekly content pack (10 post types).

- **Module 5 — Salary Negotiation**: 10 prompts (market-rate research · offer evaluation · counter-offer scripting · multiple-offer leverage · exploding offer response · deferred-start · equity / RSU · sign-on bonus · remote / relocation · justify your ask) + 8 copy-paste scripts + BATNA / comp research / walkaway math worksheets.

**All prompts tested on**: ChatGPT free tier (GPT-4o-mini / GPT-4o). Most also confirmed on Claude and Gemini.

Most updates won't require any action from you — the PDF download link auto-picks up the new version, and the Notion workspace updates live in your duplicated copy if you've left the duplication URL un-modified.

If a major rewrite happens (e.g., a new module gets added), the entry will say so, and the email you signed up for will mention it explicitly so you don't miss it.
