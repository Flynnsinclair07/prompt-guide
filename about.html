What We Do

PromptGuide is a free library of copy-paste prompts for ChatGPT, Claude, and other AI tools. Every prompt on this site is built for a specific, real-world task — writing a cover letter, planning a trip, creating a workout routine, and dozens more. You fill in the brackets with your details, paste it into your AI of choice, and get a useful response in seconds.

Why We Built This

AI tools like ChatGPT and Claude are incredibly powerful, but most people get mediocre results because they don't know how to prompt them. A good prompt can turn a generic AI response into something genuinely useful. A great prompt can save you hours of work.

We test every prompt before publishing and update them as the AI models evolve. The goal is simple: save you time and help you get better output from the tools you already use.
