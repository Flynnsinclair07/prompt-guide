## Field 5 — Description (the long body — paste as-is, Gumroad supports markdown-ish formatting)

```
Most job-hunting advice treats every step as a separate problem. Resume here. Cover letter there. Interview prep on Reddit. Salary negotiation… nowhere.

This bundle treats it as one workflow. Resume to offer letter — 5 modules, 44 copy-paste ChatGPT prompts, 8 negotiation scripts, and the worksheets you'd otherwise pay a coach $300 to walk you through.

Built from the five career prompts on snipprompts.com — expanded 2–3× with worked examples, "why this prompt works" commentary, and the salary-negotiation playbook nobody puts online for free.


## What's inside

**Module 1 — Resume**
8 prompts (resume from scratch, ATS keyword injection, bullet rewrite, career-switcher angle, executive summary, LinkedIn-to-resume convert, more) · 5-step ATS optimization checklist · 3 before/after worked examples (entry-level, mid-career switcher, senior).

**Module 2 — Cover Letter**
6 prompts (hook-first opener, mirror-the-JD, industry-switcher, referral, cold application, follow-up) · 3 plug-and-play templates · "What hiring managers actually skim for" cheat sheet (13 signals + the one question that earns the interview).

**Module 3 — Interview Prep**
12 prompts (behavioral by role, technical by industry, case interview, STAR structuring, weakness reframe, "why this company", reverse-interview, red-flag detection, more) · 3-round AI-grilling rehearsal workflow · answer-scoring prompt that grades your reply and rewrites it stronger.

**Module 4 — LinkedIn Profile**
8 prompts (headline, About section, experience bullets, skills keywording, recruiter cold-DM, recommendation request, post-connection follow-up, more) · 14-item optimization checklist · weekly content prompt pack (10 post types).

**Module 5 — Salary Negotiation** *(flagship — would be a $39 product on its own)*
10 prompts (offer evaluation, market-rate research, counter-offer scripting, multiple-offer leverage, equity / RSU, sign-on bonus, remote / relocation, exploding offer response, justify your ask, deferred-start) · 8 copy-paste scripts (decline-and-counter email, 30-second phone opener, "we don't have budget" rebuttal, "is that your final answer?" rebuttal, leverage script, accept-and-lock-terms, PTO / benefits, 30-day review revisit) · BATNA worksheet · comp research template · walkaway math calculator.


## Format

**Primary**: a 119-page PDF with a hyperlinked table of contents and every prompt in its own code-styled callout. Reads on Mac, PC, iPad, Kindle, anything.

**Bonus workspace**: an interactive Notion template with one-click copy on every prompt, for anyone who prefers a navigable workspace over a PDF.


## Who this is for

- Mid-career professionals interviewing for the next step
- Career switchers reframing transferable experience
- Senior ICs and managers negotiating offers above $120K
- New grads who want a real workflow instead of generic LinkedIn advice
- Anyone who's about to get an offer and has no idea how to counter without blowing it up


## Who this isn't for

- People who want a one-click resume generator (use Rezi or Teal)
- People looking for free prompts (snipprompts.com has 134 of them — that's the front door, this is the deep workshop)
- People who think AI can do the whole job hunt for them. The prompts are sharp; you still have to be the one who shows up.


## What you get vs. the free site

The site has 5 starter prompts on these topics. The bundle has 44 — expanded with scripts, checklists, and worked examples. Everything in Module 5 is net-new (the public site has one negotiation prompt; the bundle has ten plus eight scripts plus three worksheets).


## FAQ

**Do I need ChatGPT Plus?**
Free ChatGPT works for every prompt. A few benefit from longer context windows but none require them.

**Works with Claude or Gemini?**
Yes. The prompts are written around ChatGPT's phrasing but they run the same on Claude, Gemini, or any modern LLM.

**Is this just the prompts from snipprompts.com?**
No. The site has 5 starter prompts across these topics. The bundle has 44 — expanded, with scripts, checklists, and worked examples that aren't on the site. Module 5 (Salary Negotiation) is ~90% net-new.

**I'm a career switcher / executive / new grad — is this for me?**
All three. Every module has prompts tuned for each stage.

**PDF or Notion?**
Both. PDF is the main format. Notion is a bonus workspace if you prefer one-click copy.

**How do I get updates?**
Same Gumroad download link. Any updates within 12 months appear automatically — no re-download needed. The Notion workspace updates live.

**What about Teal, Rezi, Huntr?**
This isn't a tool — it's a workflow. Works alongside any of them.

**Refund policy?**
14 days, no questions. Gumroad handles it.
```
