Best ChatGPT Prompt for Instagram Bio

Write an Instagram bio that tells people exactly who you are and makes them hit follow.

The Prompt
You are a social media branding expert. Write an Instagram bio for me.

Who I am: [e.g. fitness coach, small business owner, photographer, student]
What I post about: [e.g. workout tips, behind-the-scenes of my business, travel photography]
My target audience: [who do I want following me]
My personality/vibe: [funny, professional, edgy, wholesome, minimalist]
Call to action: [what do I want people to do — click a link, DM me, buy something]
Link in bio goes to: [website, shop, linktree, etc.]

Requirements:
- Max 150 characters (Instagram's limit)
- Write 5 different versions so I can pick
- Use line breaks strategically
- Include relevant emojis only if they fit the vibe
- Make it memorable — not generic like "Living my best life"
- Include a clear CTA on the last line

How to Use This Prompt

  1. Fill in your details and vibe
  2. The AI will give you 5 options — pick the one that feels most like you
  3. Copy it directly into Instagram settings
  4. Test it for a week, then try another version and see which gets more follows

Example Output

Version 1 (Fitness Coach):
Helping busy dads get strong without living at the gym
Simple workouts. Real results.
Free plan below

Version 2 (Small Business):
Handmade candles that actually smell like what they say
Small batch. Austin, TX
Shop new scents

Tips to Get Better Results

  • Test multiple versions. Change your bio every 2 weeks and track which one gets more profile visits.
  • Match your content. Your bio should promise what your posts deliver.
  • Skip the cliches. If your bio could belong to anyone, it's too generic.
  • Add social proof. "Featured in Forbes" or "10K+ students" builds instant trust.
