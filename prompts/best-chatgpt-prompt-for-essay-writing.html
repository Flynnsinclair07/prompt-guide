Best ChatGPT Prompt for Essay Writing

Get a well-structured, compelling essay with a strong thesis, supporting arguments, and polished conclusion.

The Prompt
You are an expert academic writer and writing tutor. Help me write an essay.

Essay topic/question: [THE TOPIC OR PROMPT YOU NEED TO ANSWER]
Essay type: [argumentative / persuasive / expository / narrative / compare and contrast / analytical]
Word count: [REQUIRED LENGTH]
Academic level: [high school / undergraduate / graduate / professional]
Subject: [e.g. English, History, Psychology, Philosophy]
My position (if argumentative): [what side are you taking]
Sources required: [yes/no — if yes, how many]

Requirements:
- Start with a clear, debatable thesis statement
- Each body paragraph should have: topic sentence, evidence, analysis, transition
- Use formal academic tone — no contractions, no slang
- Include counterarguments and rebuttals (for argumentative essays)
- Conclusion should restate the thesis in a new way and end with a broader implication
- Vary sentence structure — mix short and long sentences
- Do NOT use phrases like "In conclusion," "Furthermore," or "It is important to note" — find more sophisticated transitions
- If I need citations, use [CITATION STYLE: MLA / APA / Chicago]

How to Use This Prompt

  1. Fill in your essay topic and requirements exactly as your professor assigned them
  2. Specify the essay type — each type has a different structure
  3. If you have a position, state it so the AI argues consistently
  4. Use the output as a strong first draft — always revise in your own voice
  5. Ask follow-ups like "Make the second paragraph stronger" or "Add a counterargument"

Important: Use AI as a writing tool, not a replacement. Most schools have AI policies — always check yours and edit heavily to make the work your own.

Example Output

Thesis: While social media platforms market themselves as tools for connection, their algorithmic design systematically prioritizes engagement over well-being, making regulation not just reasonable but necessary.

Body Paragraph 1:

The attention economy that drives social media revenue creates a fundamental misalignment between user welfare and platform profitability. Meta's own internal research, leaked in 2021, confirmed that Instagram's algorithm actively promotes content that triggers social comparison — particularly among teenage girls. When a company's profit model depends on maximizing screen time, voluntary self-regulation becomes an oxymoron. The tobacco industry's decades of self-policing offers a cautionary parallel: without external oversight, industries built on addictive products consistently choose revenue over responsibility.

Tips to Get Better Results

  • Get an outline first. Say "Give me a detailed outline before writing the full essay" — approve the structure, then ask for the full draft.
  • Strengthen weak sections. Paste a paragraph and say "This is weak — make the argument more convincing with better evidence."
  • Check for AI detection. After editing, ask "Rewrite this paragraph to sound more natural and less formulaic."
  • Ask for feedback. Paste your own draft and say "Grade this essay and tell me how to improve it."
