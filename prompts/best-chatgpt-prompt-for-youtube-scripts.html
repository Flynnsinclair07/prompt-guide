Best ChatGPT Prompt for YouTube Scripts

Write YouTube scripts that hook viewers in the first 5 seconds and keep them watching until the end.

The Prompt
You are a YouTube scriptwriter who has written for channels with 1M+ subscribers. Write a video script for me.

Video topic: [WHAT THE VIDEO IS ABOUT]
Channel niche: [e.g. tech reviews, personal finance, fitness, cooking, gaming]
Target length: [e.g. 8 minutes, 12 minutes, 20 minutes]
Target audience: [WHO WATCHES YOUR CHANNEL]
Video style: [educational / entertaining / storytelling / tutorial / review / list]
Tone: [casual / energetic / calm / professional / funny]

Requirements:
- Open with a hook in the first 5 seconds that creates curiosity or stakes
- Include a pattern interrupt every 2-3 minutes to keep retention high
- Write in a conversational, spoken tone — not essay-style
- Include [BRACKETS] for B-roll suggestions and visual cues
- Add a mid-roll CTA (subscribe, like, comment) that feels natural, not forced
- End with a strong closing that teases the next video
- Include timestamps/chapters for the description
- Write a click-worthy title (3 options) and thumbnail text idea

How to Use This Prompt

  1. Fill in your topic and channel details
  2. Specify the video length — this determines pacing and depth
  3. Read the script out loud before filming — if any line feels unnatural, ask the AI to rewrite it
  4. Use the [B-ROLL] markers to plan your visuals while editing

Example Output

Title Options:

  1. I Tried Every AI Tool for 30 Days — Here's What Actually Works
  2. Stop Wasting Money on AI Tools (Use These Instead)
  3. The Only 3 AI Tools You Actually Need in 2026

Thumbnail text: "I tested 20 AI tools" with a shocked face and crossed-out logos

[HOOK — 0:00]

"I spent $400 on AI tools last month. Most of them were useless. But three of them completely changed how I work — and two of them are free."

[B-ROLL: Screen recordings of various AI tools, quick cuts]

[INTRO — 0:15]

"I've been testing every AI tool I can find for the past 30 days. Not just trying them once — actually using them for real work..."

Tips to Get Better Results

  • Study your analytics first. Tell the AI: "My best-performing video was about [topic] — write something in that style."
  • Ask for retention hooks. Say "Add a curiosity loop at the 3-minute mark" to keep people watching.
  • Get the description too. After the script, say "Now write the YouTube description with keywords and timestamps."
  • Repurpose it. Say "Turn this script into 5 short-form clips for TikTok/Shorts" to get more content from one video.
