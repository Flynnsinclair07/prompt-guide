Best ChatGPT Prompt for Travel Planning

Get a complete day-by-day travel itinerary with activities, food, and budget — planned in seconds.

The Prompt
You are an experienced travel planner who has visited 50+ countries. Plan a trip for me.

Destination: [WHERE]
Travel dates: [START DATE to END DATE]
Number of travelers: [NUMBER] — [e.g. couple, family with kids, solo, group of friends]
Budget: $[TOTAL AMOUNT] (not including flights)
Travel style: [budget backpacker / mid-range comfort / luxury / adventure]
Interests: [e.g. food, history, nightlife, nature, shopping, art, beaches, hiking]
Must-dos: [anything specific you definitely want to do]
Avoid: [anything you don't want — e.g. tourist traps, long drives, museums]
Hotel preference: [hostel / Airbnb / boutique hotel / resort]

Requirements:
- Create a day-by-day itinerary with morning, afternoon, and evening plans
- Include specific restaurant recommendations for each day (not just "find a local place")
- Estimate costs for each day (hotel, food, activities, transport)
- Include travel time between activities so the schedule is realistic
- Add one "hidden gem" per day that most tourists miss
- Include practical tips: best transport options, what to book in advance, what to skip
- End with a packing checklist specific to this trip

How to Use This Prompt

  1. Fill in your destination and dates
  2. Be specific about your interests — "food and nightlife" gets a very different trip than "hiking and nature"
  3. Include your budget so the recommendations actually match what you can spend
  4. After getting the itinerary, ask follow-ups like "What if it rains on day 3?" or "Give me backup options for day 5"

Example Output

Day 1 — Arrival in Lisbon

  • Morning: Land at LIS, take the metro to Baixa-Chiado (€1.50, 25 min). Check into Casa do Príncipe (boutique hotel, ~€95/night)
  • Afternoon: Walk through Alfama neighborhood. Stop at Miradouro da Graça for the best free viewpoint in the city. Skip the Castelo de São Jorge (€10, overcrowded) — the view from outside is just as good
  • Evening: Dinner at Taberna da Rua das Flores (reservations required, ~€25/person). Try the octopus rice. Then walk to Time Out Market for a drink
  • Hidden gem: Fado in Tasca do Chico — tiny bar, no cover, authentic fado music starting at 9pm. Arrive by 8:30 or you won't get a seat

Day 1 estimated cost: €145 (hotel €95 + food €35 + transport €15)

Tips to Get Better Results

  • Ask for alternatives. Say "Give me a rainy day version of this itinerary" for backup plans.
  • Get a booking checklist. Ask "What should I book in advance vs. walk up?" so you don't miss out.
  • Compare destinations. Not sure where to go? Ask "Compare 5 days in Lisbon vs Barcelona for a couple who likes food and nightlife, budget $2000."
  • Ask about timing. "Is [month] a good time to visit [destination]?" saves you from bad weather or peak crowds.
