Best ChatGPT Prompt for Amazon FBA

Use AI to research products, write optimized listings, and plan your Amazon FBA strategy.

The Prompt
You are an Amazon FBA consultant who has launched 50+ products and generated $10M+ in revenue. Help me with my Amazon business.

What I need help with: [pick one]
- Product research: Help me evaluate whether [PRODUCT IDEA] is worth selling
- Listing optimization: Rewrite my listing for [PRODUCT] to rank higher and convert better
- PPC strategy: Help me plan my advertising for [PRODUCT]
- Launch strategy: Create a 30-day launch plan for [PRODUCT]

Product details (if applicable):
- Product: [WHAT YOU'RE SELLING]
- Category: [AMAZON CATEGORY]
- Price point: $[AMOUNT]
- Main competitors: [LIST 2-3 COMPETITOR ASINS OR PRODUCTS]
- What makes yours different: [YOUR UNIQUE ANGLE]

For listing optimization, include:
- Title (under 200 chars, keyword-rich, readable)
- 5 bullet points (features + benefits, front-loaded with keywords)
- Product description (HTML formatted, 1000+ words)
- Backend search terms (250 bytes max, no repeats from title/bullets)
- A+ Content outline if Brand Registered

Requirements:
- Use real Amazon SEO best practices, not generic advice
- Include specific keyword placement strategy
- If evaluating a product, give me a honest go/no-go recommendation with reasons

How to Use This Prompt

  1. Pick one focus area per session — don't try to do everything at once
  2. For product research, include competitor prices and review counts for realistic analysis
  3. For listing optimization, paste your current listing so the AI can improve it
  4. Always validate AI keyword suggestions with a real tool like Helium 10 or Jungle Scout

Example Output

Task: Listing optimization for silicone kitchen tongs

Title: Kitchen Tongs for Cooking — 12-Inch Silicone-Tipped, Heat Resistant to 480°F, Locking Mechanism, Non-Stick Safe | Stainless Steel BBQ & Salad Tongs (2-Pack, Black)

Bullet 1: HEAT RESISTANT TO 480°F — Cook, grill, and serve with confidence. Our BPA-free silicone tips won't melt, scratch your pans, or transfer heat to your hands. Safe for cast iron, non-stick, stainless steel, and ceramic cookware.

Bullet 2: ONE-HAND LOCKING MECHANISM — Pull the ring to lock them closed for compact drawer storage. No more tongs springing open in your utensil drawer. Opens smoothly with a single thumb push.

Tips to Get Better Results

  • Paste competitor listings. Say "Here are my top 3 competitors' listings — write mine to be better."
  • Ask for review analysis. Paste competitor reviews and say "What are customers complaining about? How should I position my product?"
  • Get PPC keywords. After the listing, say "Give me 50 PPC keywords grouped by match type with suggested bids."
  • Seasonal strategy. Ask "What should I change in my listing and PPC for Q4/holiday season?"
