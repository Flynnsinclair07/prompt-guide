Best ChatGPT Prompt for Social Media Captions

Scroll-stopping captions for Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, and TikTok — in seconds.

The Prompt 
You are a social media copywriter who specializes in viral, high-engagement content. Write a caption for me.

Platform: [Instagram / LinkedIn / Twitter / TikTok]
What the post is about: [describe the photo, video, or topic]
Goal: [engagement / sales / brand awareness / educate / entertain]
Target audience: [who should care about this post]
Brand voice: [professional / casual / funny / inspirational / edgy]
Include hashtags: [yes / no]
Include a call to action: [yes — what action? e.g. "comment below", "link in bio", "share this"]

Requirements:
- Hook in the first line — it needs to stop the scroll
- Keep it platform-appropriate (short for Twitter, longer for LinkedIn, etc.)
- No generic phrases like "check this out" or "exciting news"
- Write 3 different versions so I can pick the best one
- Include relevant hashtags if requested (max 10 for Instagram, 3-5 for LinkedIn)

How to Use This Prompt

  1. Copy the prompt and fill in your details
  2. Be specific about the post content — "photo of my new product on a kitchen counter" gets way better results than "product photo"
  3. Picking the right platform matters — LinkedIn captions are nothing like Instagram captions
  4. Paste into ChatGPT or Claude
  5. Pick your favorite version, tweak it, post it

Example Output

Platform: Instagram
Post: Photo of handmade ceramic mug
Voice: Casual/warm

Every piece is hand-thrown, glazed twice, and completely one-of-a-kind. Yours won't look exactly like anyone else's — that's the point.

Link in bio if you want one before this batch is gone.

Handmade. Hand-glazed. Holds 12oz of whatever gets you through the morning.

Shop link in bio. These sell out fast and I'm not just saying that.

#ceramics #handmademug #coffeelover #pottery #supportsmallbusiness

Tips to Get Better Results

  • Study what works. Before using the prompt, look at top posts in your niche. Tell the AI: "Write in the style of [account you admire]."
  • Test hooks. Ask for "5 different first lines" separately — the hook is 80% of whether someone reads the rest.
  • Batch create. Say "Write captions for 7 posts this week" and give it all your content ideas at once.
  • Repurpose. After getting an Instagram caption, say "Now adapt this for LinkedIn" — saves time and keeps messaging consistent.
