Best ChatGPT Prompt for Product Descriptions

Write product descriptions that convert browsers into buyers — for Amazon, Shopify, Etsy, or any store.

The Prompt
You are an ecommerce copywriter who specializes in high-converting product descriptions. Write a product description for me.

Product: [PRODUCT NAME]
Category: [e.g. skincare, electronics, clothing, home decor]
Platform: [Amazon / Shopify / Etsy / other]
Price point: $[AMOUNT]
Target customer: [WHO BUYS THIS — age, lifestyle, pain point they have]
Key features: [LIST 5-7 FEATURES]
What makes it different: [what sets it apart from competitors]
Materials/specs: [relevant technical details]

Requirements:
- Write a compelling title (optimized for search on the platform)
- Opening paragraph that sells the benefit, not just the feature
- 5 bullet points highlighting key features with benefits
- A short paragraph about who this product is perfect for
- Include relevant keywords naturally for SEO
- Tone should match the brand: [premium / casual / fun / technical / luxurious]
- Keep the total description under 300 words

How to Use This Prompt

  1. Fill in your product details — include everything the buyer would want to know
  2. Specify the platform because Amazon, Shopify, and Etsy have different formats
  3. Include your target customer so the language speaks directly to them
  4. Paste into ChatGPT or Claude and copy the output into your listing

Example Output

Title: Bamboo Laptop Stand — Adjustable, Ergonomic Desk Riser for MacBook & PC | Natural Wood Finish

Stop hunching over your laptop. This handcrafted bamboo stand raises your screen to eye level, turning any desk into an ergonomic workstation — without the plastic eyesore of most laptop risers.

  • Adjustable height: 6 angle settings from 15° to 45° so you find your perfect viewing position
  • Solid bamboo construction: Naturally antimicrobial and 3x stronger than most hardwoods — no wobble, no flex
  • Built-in ventilation: Cutout design improves airflow, keeping your laptop up to 12°F cooler
  • Universal fit: Holds laptops 10" to 17" including MacBook Pro, Dell XPS, and ThinkPad
  • Cable management: Rear channel keeps your charging cable routed and tidy

Tips to Get Better Results

  • Study your competitors. Paste a competitor's listing and say "Write something better than this that highlights [your advantage]."
  • Ask for A/B versions. Say "Give me two versions — one emotional, one technical" and test which converts better.
  • Batch them. If you have 20 products, give the AI your template and say "Now write descriptions for these 5 products" with specs for each.
  • Add social proof. Tell the AI: "Include a line referencing that we have 500+ five-star reviews."
