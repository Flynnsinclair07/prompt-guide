Best ChatGPT Prompt for Writing a Resume

Turn your experience into a clean, ATS-friendly resume that actually gets read by hiring managers.

The Prompt 
You are a professional resume writer with 15 years of experience helping people land jobs at top companies. Write a resume for me.

Target job title: [JOB TITLE YOU'RE APPLYING FOR]
Industry: [INDUSTRY]

My experience:
- Job 1: [TITLE] at [COMPANY], [DATES]. Key responsibilities: [WHAT YOU DID]. Key achievement: [BIGGEST WIN WITH NUMBERS]
- Job 2: [TITLE] at [COMPANY], [DATES]. Key responsibilities: [WHAT YOU DID]. Key achievement: [BIGGEST WIN WITH NUMBERS]
- Job 3: [TITLE] at [COMPANY], [DATES]. Key responsibilities: [WHAT YOU DID]. Key achievement: [BIGGEST WIN WITH NUMBERS]

Education: [DEGREE, SCHOOL, YEAR]
Skills: [LIST YOUR SKILLS]

Requirements:
- Use strong action verbs (led, built, increased, reduced, launched)
- Quantify achievements with numbers wherever possible
- Keep it to one page
- Make it ATS-friendly — no tables, columns, or graphics
- Write a 2-sentence professional summary at the top
- Focus on results and impact, not just duties
- Use bullet points, not paragraphs

How to Use This Prompt

  1. Copy the prompt and fill in every [BRACKET] with your real info
  2. Include at least 2-3 jobs. More detail = better resume
  3. Always include numbers — revenue, percentages, team sizes, anything measurable
  4. Paste into ChatGPT or Claude
  5. Copy the output into Google Docs or your favorite resume template

If you don't have exact numbers, estimate. "Managed a team of ~10" is way better than "managed a team."

Example Output

SARAH CHEN
sarah.chen@email.com | (555) 123-4567 | linkedin.com/in/sarahchen

Professional Summary
Results-driven marketing manager with 7 years of experience scaling B2B SaaS growth from startup to $20M ARR. Proven track record of building demand generation engines that consistently deliver 3x pipeline targets.

Experience

Senior Marketing Manager — Acme SaaS, 2022-Present

  • Built and led a 5-person demand gen team that grew marketing-sourced pipeline from $2M to $8M quarterly
  • Launched ABM program targeting enterprise accounts, resulting in 45% higher close rates
  • Reduced customer acquisition cost by 30% through channel optimization and improved targeting

Tips to Get Better Results

  • Tailor it to each job. Tell the AI the exact job title you're applying for so it uses the right keywords.
  • Add the job description. Append "Here's the job posting: [paste it]" and ask the AI to align your resume to it.
  • Ask for variations. Say "Give me 3 different versions of the professional summary" and pick the best one.
  • Check for ATS keywords. Ask the AI: "What keywords from this job posting should I include in my resume?"
