## Image production notes

- All pin images should be 1000 x 1500 px (Pinterest's recommended 2:3 ratio)
- For PDF screenshots: open in Preview, take Cmd+Shift+4 region screenshot at 200% zoom for crispness
- Add a thin black border + the title text overlaid in white for contrast (Canva or Figma works)
- Brand-consistent: black backgrounds, white text, sans-serif font (matches PDF cover)
