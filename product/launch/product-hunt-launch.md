## Maker's first comment (FINAL)

```
hey Product Hunt,

I kept using ChatGPT for real tasks — resumes, cover letters, emails — and kept getting back confident garbage. Invented metrics I never gave it. Phrases no human says. Output that screamed "an AI wrote this."

It wasn't ChatGPT's fault — it was the prompts. Most prompt packs are one-liners that ask for output without asking for context first.

So I built SnipPrompts: 134 free, tested prompts engineered around how generic AI actually fails:
- they refuse to invent numbers — no specifics from you, they ask first
- banned-phrase lists that kill the AI-tells
- post-output rules: what to do AFTER you get the draft

Everything on the site is free to copy — no signup, no wall. Works with ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini.

There's also a paid bundle for job hunters who want the deep version (44 prompts + negotiation scripts + worksheets) — but the 134 free prompts are the real front door. Start there.

One thing I'm curious about — what's the worst AI-generated output you've gotten? An invented statistic, a cliché, something that just screamed "a bot wrote this"? The prompts are built around those exact failure modes, and I'd love to know if what I designed around matches what you've actually hit.
```
