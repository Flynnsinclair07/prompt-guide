## 1. Module breakdown

### Module 1 — Resume
- **8 prompts**: resume from scratch · resume rewrite · bullet rewrite · ATS keyword injection · quantify achievements · career-switcher angle · executive summary · LinkedIn-to-resume convert
- **ATS optimization workflow**: 5-step checklist (keyword density, section order, formatting, file type, scan test)
- **3 before/after worked examples**: entry-level · mid-career switcher · senior

### Module 2 — Cover Letter
- **6 prompts**: hook-first opener · mirror-the-JD · industry-switcher version · referral version · cold application · follow-up
- **3 templates**: 1-page classic · story-driven · brief-and-punchy
- **"What hiring managers actually skim for"** — 1-page cheat sheet

### Module 3 — Interview Prep
- **12 prompts**: behavioral Qs by role · technical Qs by industry · case interview · STAR answer structuring · weakness reframe · "why this company" research · closing questions to ask · salary screen · panel prep · post-interview thank-you · **reverse-interview (screen the company for red flags)** · **red-flag detection (paste interviewer's answers/behavior, AI flags warning signs)**
- **AI-grilling rehearsal workflow**: 3-round structure (warm-up · pressure · curveballs)
- **Answer-scoring prompt**: user pastes their answer, AI rates it, rewrites stronger

### Module 4 — LinkedIn Profile
- **8 prompts**: headline · about section · experience bullets · skills section keywording · engagement-seed posts · **recruiter cold-DM** (reach-out when you see an open role) · **recommendation-request** (ask former managers/colleagues without feeling weird) · **post-connection follow-up** (what to send after they accept)
- **Optimization checklist**: 14 items (URL, banner, photo, headline, etc.)
- **Weekly content ideas prompt pack**: 10 post types

### Module 5 — Salary Negotiation *(flagship module — mostly net-new)*
- **10 prompts**: offer evaluation · market-rate research · counter-offer scripting · justify your ask · multiple-offer leverage · deferred-start negotiation · **equity / RSU negotiation** · **sign-on bonus negotiation** · **remote / relocation negotiation** · **exploding offer response**
- **8 copy-paste scripts**:
  - Email: decline first offer politely + counter
  - Phone: 30-second opener for salary discussion
  - Rebuttal: "We don't have budget"
  - Rebuttal: "Is that your final answer?"
  - Accept: lock in final terms
  - **Leverage: "I have another offer" script**
  - **PTO / benefits negotiation script**
  - **30-day review revisit script (for when you accept below ask)**
- **3 worksheets**:
  - Walkaway math (benefit-equivalency calculator)
  - **BATNA worksheet** (map your Best Alternative To Negotiated Agreement)
  - **Comp research template** (pull Levels.fyi, Glassdoor, Blind data into a defensible ask)

**Module 5 positioning**: should feel like a $39 product on its own. Most buyers will open the bundle *for* this module — don't short-change it.

**Totals**: 44 prompts · 8 scripts · 4 workflows · 3 checklists · 3 worked examples · 3 worksheets

## 4. Format & navigation

### PDF (primary format)
The polished, delivered product — what buyers see first in the Gumroad download.

- Single PDF file (~60–80 pages)
- Navigable TOC with hyperlinked module jumps
- Every prompt in a code-styled callout box with clear visual separation
- Print-ready (letter-size, readable margins)
- Module intros + "when to use this" framing at the start of each section

### Notion template (bonus workspace — power-user tier)
Positioned as "for power users who want one-click copy and a navigable workspace."

Left sidebar, nested toggles:
```
📘 Job Hunter's AI Bundle
├── Start here (5-min welcome)
├── 01 Resume
│   ├── Prompts (8) · ATS workflow · Before/after examples
├── 02 Cover Letter
│   ├── Prompts (6) · Templates (3) · Skim-for cheat sheet
├── 03 Interview Prep
│   ├── Prompts (12) · Rehearsal workflow · Answer scorer
├── 04 LinkedIn Profile
│   ├── Prompts (8) · Optimization checklist · Weekly content pack
├── 05 Salary Negotiation
│   ├── Prompts (10) · Scripts (8) · BATNA · Comp research · Walkaway math
├── Quick reference card (1-page index)
└── Updates log
```

**UX rules**:
- Every Notion prompt lives in a code block with a visible copy button
- Each module opens with a 3-sentence "when to use this" intro
- No cross-links to snipprompts.com from inside prompts (bundle stands alone; external links only in welcome page + updates log)
- Users duplicate the template to their own workspace (optional; view-only copy still works)
- Framing in welcome page: "The PDF is your main reference. Notion is here if you prefer a navigable workspace with one-click copy."

## 6. Cover design direction

- **1200×1600** (Gumroad product cover, reads at 200px thumbnail)
- **Palette**: navy `#1B2A4E` + cream `#F5EBDD` (same family as pins for brand continuity)
- **Distinct from pin aesthetic** — this is the paid product, not a lead magnet. No Pinterest-style checkmark lists.

**Layout**:
- **Headline** top third: serif display font (e.g. Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond, or similar — not Arial Black), cream on navy, mixed case ("The Job Hunter's / AI Bundle")
- **Accent under headline**: thin cream horizontal rule, optional cream "seal" or badge with "The Job Hunter's Bundle" lockup
- **Mid-band**: 5 module chips in a single horizontal strip (cream text on deeper navy, spaced) — `RESUME · COVER LETTER · INTERVIEW · LINKEDIN · NEGOTIATION`
- **Bottom**: tagline (e.g. "Resume to offer letter in one workflow") + `snipprompts.com` in small caps
- **Optional**: subtle cream grain/texture overlay (1–2% opacity) for depth

**Rules**:
- No photos, no people, no cartoon icons
- No checkmarks or benefit lists (Pinterest pin territory, not cover)
- Looks like a book cover, not a free download

## 8. Pricing rationale — why $39

**Anchors**:
- 1-hour career coach: $150–400

- Gumroad prompt packs: $9–19 (commodity tier — what we're escaping)

$39 sits above the commodity tier (signals quality) and below the $50 "pause and think" threshold for active job seekers (they're outcome-focused, not price-focused).

## Risks + open questions

- **Notion duplication UX**: copy-to-clipboard works in view-only mode; test before ship.
- **Salary Negotiation module is flagship**: highest value, highest writing load. Carve ≥4–5 hrs specifically.
- **Cover design** must feel like a book, not a pin. Commit to a serif display font and evaluate against the pins side-by-side before shipping.
- **Anti-piracy**: don't worry v1. Gumroad watermarks; pirates aren't the target buyer anyway.
- **12-month updates promise**: track a simple changelog in the Notion + PDF so updates are visible; don't over-commit to frequency.
